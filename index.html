Will they deliver on time?

A new food delivery service delivers food in a matter of seconds! But it needs your help to make it happen. You don't want to deliver the food too early or it may get cold and not taste as great. Obviously, don't take too long to deliver it!

How to play

  1. Click the set button below to generate your target delivery time. You must deliver the food within 0.5 seconds of that time - no earlier, no later.
  2. Start the clock by clicking on the start button.
  3. Stop the clock by clicking on the stop button.
  4. Play again by setting a new target delivery time.
